Hi! I'm Adele

Welcome to my online portfolio! I'm a product design student at NMIMS School of Design. Here, you'll find a showcase of my design work, reflecting my passion for innovative and user-centered design solutions. Explore my portfolio to discover how I blend creativity and functionality to create meaningful product experiences.

Coral Oasis

"Coral Oasis" is a stunning seating concept, drawing inspiration from the intricate beauty of coral formations. These circular white capsule seats not only add a touch of artistry to office and mall foyer areas but also provide a unique blend of privacy and supreme comfort for a tranquil and inviting environment.

Wood Print

"Wood Print" is a complete design project that introduces an eco-friendly children's puzzle. It includes the puzzle itself and its unique branding, all with a focus on creativity and being kind to the environment.

Seashell Symphony

The "Seashell Symphony" is a singular creation, sculpted to mimic the graceful form of a seashell. With an intricately patterned side and a top reminiscent of a pristine shell, this block combines the beauty of nature with functional design, making it an ideal choice for interior decor.
